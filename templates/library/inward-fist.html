Inward Fist

Body Training

Hands

An inward fist's hands are his weapons. Training hands increases the damage dealt by the character for all weapon attacks.

NOTE: This does not affect the fist's energy attacks.

Torso

An inward fist's torso is the core of its defense. Training torso increases resistance to attacks. It represents a form of flat damage reduction.

Legs

An inward fist's legs are used as an alternate weapon. Training legs increases damage with kicks significantly, and makes ki-building kick moves like spinkick and shinkick more effective.

Arms

An inward fist's arms can be trained to increase elusiveness. Training arms increases the character's chances of blocking incoming attacks.

Disciplines

Discipine is split into 10 levels, with 6 abilities spread out between them. This means that sometimes, training a single discipline level does not grant access to a new ability.

1 Discipline: Truesight

By training their senses and awareness of the energies of chakra, the inward fist can detect things not seen by the physical eye alone.

4 Discipline: Levitation

Through focus, determination and sheer inner will, the inward fist can lift themselves from the shackles of the earth.

5 Discipline: Roundhouse

A simple but effective attack that can hit multiple enemies.

7 Discipline: Inner Fire

By turning their focus inward and allowing their ki to flow through their body, the inward fist can burn away impurities in the body.

8 Discipline: Pheonix Fire

The inward fist can train themselves to redirect their flow of ki, empowering their attacks at the cost of their speed.

9 Discipline: Dim-Mak

Using their knowledge of Ki in all things, the inward fist can use their own energy to throw off the alignment of energy in others, essentially poisoning their energy.

Ki

Ki represents the character's ability to generate and focus inner energies. It is split into 30 levels. Each Ki level from 10-30 represents a single combo attack. In combat, ki is built by using ki-building moves.
